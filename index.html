Botto — project status update
Overview Last updated: April 21, 2026

This is the first edition of a bi-weekly project status update from the Botto core team. Going forward, this page will be updated every two weeks and shared with the DAO as our primary transparency tool for tracking what we're working on, what's coming next, and what has been delivered.

Our work is currently organized around two pillars: sanctifying the core — making sure Botto's protocol and operations are solid, sustainable, and well-documented — and enabling growth, which includes governance improvements, new curation mechanics, and opening the platform to outside builders. Everything you see below sits under one of these two pillars.

This first report covers the full current project landscape across seven teams: Agent, Platform, DAO, Operations, Marketing, Studio, and Sales. Future editions will open with a short summary of what changed since the last update. Projects listed as Ongoing (core) are the baseline work that keeps Botto running every week regardless of special projects. All other projects have a defined milestone and deadline.
